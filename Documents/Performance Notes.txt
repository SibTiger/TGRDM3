Performance Notes:
====================

Most maps in this project uses seriously intensive resources for the features\eye-candy used.  Toggle these Console Variables:


    Line Mirrors
        R_DrawMirrors [True|False]
    Sector Reflections
        GL_Plane_Reflection [True|False]
    Dynamic Lights
        GL_Lights [True|False]
    Voxels
        R_DrawVoxels [True|False]
